◈ Trading Strategy

Gold XAU/USD

Price action setups, entry rules, and risk management for the $5→$1,000 journey.

◆
Why Gold?
XAU/USD offers high volatility, tight spreads in sessions, clear technical levels, and responds well to price action. Micro lots let you trade with small capital while keeping risk controlled.
🎯
Core Philosophy
Wait for the market to come to you. Only trade at key levels with a clear trigger. No trades in noise. One good setup per day beats three average ones.
⚖️
Risk Per Trade
Risk 1–2% of capital per trade maximum. With $5, that's $0.05–$0.10 per trade. As capital grows, lot size scales up — never risk more than 2% regardless.
1
Market Structure
Always identify the trend before looking for entries
Bullish Market Structure
UPTREND
Higher Highs + Higher Lows = Uptrend. Only look for BUY setups. Each pullback to a Higher Low is a potential entry zone. Do not sell against a confirmed uptrend.
Bearish Market Structure
DOWNTREND
Lower Highs + Lower Lows = Downtrend. Only look for SELL setups. Each bounce to a Lower High is a potential short entry. Do not buy against a confirmed downtrend.
2
Support & Resistance Levels
The foundation of every trade — price respects these zones repeatedly
Key S/R Zones on XAU/USD — H1 Example
GOLD LEVELS
How to use S/R: Mark zones where price has reversed at least 2–3 times. The more touches, the stronger the level. Trade the reaction at the level, not in advance. Wait for a confirmation candle before entering.
3
Entry Setups
Three high-probability patterns that repeat on Gold daily
Setup A — Pin Bar Reversal
BUY & SELL
A Pin Bar has a small body with a long wick that pierces through a key level and closes back. The long wick shows strong rejection. Enter on the close of the pin bar candle. Stop loss goes beyond the wick tip.
Setup B — Break & Retest (BnR)
HIGH PROBABILITY
The Break & Retest is one of the most reliable setups on Gold. Old resistance flips to new support (and vice versa). Wait for price to retest the broken level and show a rejection candle. Enter with a tight stop below the level.
Setup C — Inside Bar Breakout
CONTINUATION
An Inside Bar represents a pause / compression in the trend. The smaller bar is fully contained within the prior bar's range. Enter when price breaks the mother bar in the direction of the trend. Target R:R of at least 2:1.
4
Risk Management
Protect the capital — this is what separates winners from losers
Reward:Risk Ratio — Why 2:1 Minimum?
With a 2:1 R:R, you only need to win 34% of trades to break even. At 50% win rate you profit significantly. Never take a trade below 1.5:1.

🛑
Always Use a Stop Loss
Place SL immediately on entry. No exceptions. On Gold, minimum 15–20 pips above/below the level to avoid stop hunting. Never move SL against your trade.
🎯
Set TP Before Entry
Know your target before clicking Buy/Sell. Target the next key S/R level. Partial profit at 1:1, move SL to breakeven, let rest run to 2:1 or beyond.
📏
Position Sizing
Calculate lot size based on SL distance and account risk %. With $5 capital and 2% risk = $0.10 max loss per trade. Use the Lot Calculator in Real Time tab.
🚫
Max 1 Trade at a Time
While account is under $100, only carry one open position. Overlapping trades compound drawdown. Focus on quality over quantity — one perfect trade per day is enough.
📉
Daily Loss Limit
Stop trading after losing 3–5% of capital in one day. Come back tomorrow fresh. Revenge trading after losses is the #1 account killer — hard rule, no exceptions.
🔄
Breakeven + Trail
Once price moves 1R in your favor, move SL to entry (breakeven). Then trail the stop using structure — behind each new HL in an uptrend, locking in profit as it forms.
