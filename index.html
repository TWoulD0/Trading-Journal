◈ Trading Strategy

Gold XAU/USD

Price action setups, entry rules, and risk management for the $5→$1,000 journey.

◆
Why Gold?
XAU/USD offers high volatility, tight spreads in sessions, clear technical levels, and responds well to price action. Micro lots let you trade with small capital while keeping risk controlled.
🎯
Core Philosophy
Wait for the market to come to you. Only trade at key levels with a clear trigger. No trades in noise. One good setup per day beats three average ones.
⚖️
Risk Per Trade
Risk 1–2% of capital per trade maximum. With $5, that's $0.05–$0.10 per trade. As capital grows, lot size scales up — never risk more than 2% regardless.
1
Market Structure
Always identify the trend before looking for entries
Bullish Market Structure
UPTREND
Higher Highs + Higher Lows = Uptrend. Only look for BUY setups. Each pullback to a Higher Low is a potential entry zone.
Bearish Market Structure
DOWNTREND
Lower Highs + Lower Lows = Downtrend. Only look for SELL setups. Each bounce to a Lower High is a potential short entry.
4
Risk Management
Protect the capital — this is what separates winners from losers
Reward:Risk Ratio — Why 2:1 Minimum?
With a 2:1 R:R, you only need to win 34% of trades to break even. At 50% win rate you profit significantly.

🛑
Always Use a Stop Loss
Place SL immediately on entry. No exceptions. On Gold, minimum 15–20 pips above/below the level to avoid stop hunting.
🎯
Set TP Before Entry
Know your target before clicking Buy/Sell. Target the next key S/R level. Partial profit at 1:1, move SL to breakeven, let rest run.
🚫
Max 1 Trade at a Time
While account is under $100, only carry one open position. Overlapping trades compound drawdown.
📉
Daily Loss Limit
Stop trading after losing 3–5% of capital in one day. Come back tomorrow fresh. Revenge trading is the #1 account killer.
